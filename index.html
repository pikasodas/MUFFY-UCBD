Program overview

The MUFY Standard Program is a comprehensive pre university course designed to prepare students for undergraduate study in Australia. Learn from experienced, Monash approved faculty at UCBD, gain essential academic skills, and enjoy a seamless transition to Monash University with support at every stage.
